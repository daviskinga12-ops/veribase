The problem
Every Kenyan business
operates blind.
No shared staff history
A cashier who stole at their last 3 jobs gets hired again tomorrow. No record exists anywhere.
No supplier accountability
You pay a supplier who has defrauded 12 other businesses. You have no way to know.
No credit intelligence
You extend credit to a business that has defaulted on 5 others this year. No warning exists.
Invisible to capital
Your business earns KSh 3M/month. To a bank, you do not exist. No verifiable track record.
Root cause
"Every problem in Kenyan business traces to one cause: asymmetry of truth. Some people see reality clearly. Most don't. And the ones who do — extract value from the ones who don't."
This is not a product problem. It is a coordination infrastructure problem.
The solution
We build the missing layer.
A shared protocol of economic truth that makes Kenya's invisible economy visible — to businesses, banks, and the world.

Go to market
Cold start solved.
Month 1–2
Seed with public data
Manually build profiles for 500 Nairobi businesses using BRS, KRA, court records. Day-one value before first signup.
Month 2–4
Anchor tenant strategy
Partner with 1 business association or SACCO (target: Kenya National Chamber of Commerce). Their 300+ members join overnight.
Month 4–8
Network effects kick in
Each new business that joins makes it more valuable for others. Growth becomes organic. Begin bank conversations.
Month 8–12
First institutional deal
Close first bank or fintech API licensing deal. This de-risks the business model and accelerates growth.
