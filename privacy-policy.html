Veribase Limited ("Veribase", "we", "us", or "our") is registered as a Data Controller/Processor under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC). We are committed to protecting your personal data and your rights under Kenyan law.

2. What Data We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address (when joining the waitlist or registering)
  • Full name and business name
  • Business registration number (KRA PIN, BRS number)
  • Phone number
  • Business category and location
  • Supplier, staff, and transaction records you choose to log

Information collected automatically

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the platform
  • Referral source (how you found Veribase)

Information from third parties

  • Business registration data from the Business Registration Service (BRS)
  • KRA PIN verification status (with your consent)
  • Public court and litigation records

3. Why We Process Your Data (Legal Basis)

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, we process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Contract performance — to provide the Veribase service to you
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our platform and prevent fraud
  • Consent — for marketing communications (you may withdraw at any time)
  • Legal obligation — to comply with Kenyan law, including tax and anti-money laundering requirements

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your Veribase account
  • To build and maintain verified business profiles
  • To enable trust lookups between businesses on the platform
  • To send you product updates, security alerts, and platform notifications
  • To send marketing communications (only with your explicit consent)
  • To provide aggregated, anonymised market intelligence to institutional partners
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations
  • To detect and prevent fraud and platform abuse

5. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

Other Veribase users

Verified business profile information is visible to other registered businesses on the platform, consistent with the core purpose of the service. You control what information is included in your profile.

Institutional partners (banks, fintechs)

Aggregated and anonymised economic intelligence data may be shared with licensed financial institutions. Individual personal data is only shared with your explicit written consent.

Service providers

We use third-party service providers for hosting (Vercel), database (Supabase), and email delivery. All providers are contractually bound to process data only on our instructions.

Legal requirements

We may disclose data when required by Kenyan law, court order, or government authority.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers located in Europe (Vercel/Supabase infrastructure). We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Role-based access controls limiting internal access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Audit logs for all data access events

In the event of a personal data breach, we will notify the ODPC within 72 hours and affected users without undue delay, as required by the Data Protection Act.

7. Your Rights Under the Data Protection Act, 2019

As a data subject under Kenyan law, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time, for consent-based processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@veribase.co. We will respond within 21 days.

If you are unsatisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) at www.odpc.go.ke.

8. Data Retention

  • Waitlist email addresses: until you request deletion or 24 months after collection
  • Business account data: for the duration of your account plus 7 years (statutory requirement)
  • Transaction and reputation records: 5 years from the date of the record
  • Server logs: 90 days

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — these are required for the platform to function and cannot be disabled. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

10. Children's Privacy

Veribase is a business platform intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, contact us immediately at privacy@veribase.co.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or Kenyan law. We will notify registered users of material changes via email at least 14 days before they take effect. The current version is always available at veribase.co/privacy-policy.
